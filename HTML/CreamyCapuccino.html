Creamy Cappuccino

Description

Our Creamy Cappuccino combines a perfect shot of espresso with silky steamed milk and a luxurious layer of milk foam. The result is a harmonious blend of rich coffee flavor and creamy texture that will delight your taste buds.

Specifications

  • Base: Double shot of espresso
  • Milk: Fresh whole milk, steamed to perfection
  • Foam: Rich microfoam layer
  • Serving Size: 6 oz (180ml)
